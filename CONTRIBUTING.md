## Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Please read and follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to help maintain a positive environment.

### Core Values
- **Respect**: Treat all contributors with dignity
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Transparency**: Be open and honest in communication
- **Excellence**: Strive for quality in code and collaboration
